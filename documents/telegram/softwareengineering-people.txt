User_bc9342: hello everyone 👋

I built a browser extension called Arbor 🌳 and wanted to share it here with you to get early insights and feedback.

The problem it’s trying to solve is something I kept running into mid-chat: you’re deep into a conversation, you suddenly think of an important side question, and you hesitate.

• Do you ask it now and flood the chat with side topics?
• Or ignore it and risk losing a valuable direction entirely?

Arbor helps you organize LLM conversations and the relationships between them as a visual mind map. Instead of one long, messy chat or a bunch of disconnected conversations, each chat becomes a node, and follow-up questions branch off from it. You can literally see how ideas connect and how your thinking evolves over time. 

If you use chatbots regularly, I’d love for you to try it and tell me what you think. It's currently working in ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Perplexity chat interfaces

👉 Extension link
https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/arbor-chat-tree-navigator/dkkjcehlajiahkjabnjoklnahblnlndi

Any feedback (confusing, broken, or cool) is very welcome 🙂
