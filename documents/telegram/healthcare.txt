User_52ac9c: How to submit Cigna Claims (reimbursement for medical bills)

For those wondering how to submit a claim to Cigna:

1 - go to this website: customer.cignaenvoy.com

2 - Log into your account (inform your Cigna ID (it's in your member ID card that they sent by email) and the password you created the first time you logged in)

3 - Put the mouse over the button "Claims" without clicking on it --> then click on "Submit a new claim"

4 - Inform date of treatment, diagnosis, symptoms, and send photos/documents of what's relevant. Like medical letters, prescriptions, receipts, etc. Example: if you're submitting a claim for reimbursement for medicine, submit the prescription and the receipt.

5 - Inform your bank details. Important: they will ask for your Bank address (not YOUR address). Be aware if they say "bank city" "bank address", etc --> this means it's your bank address that they want. To find your bank address, search on your bank's website how to receive wire transfers, they usually have this info there. Here's the Capital One info as an example: https://www.capitalone.com/help-center/checking-savings/send-receive-wire-transfer/

6 - Then you will inform your bank account's details, such as account number and ABA number ("ABA number" is your "routing number") - you can find this information in your bank's app! Make sure you get the details for the account you would like to receive the reimbursement in (e.g. if you have both checking and savings accounts with the same bank, they will have different details, account numbers, etc - get the details of the account you wish to receive the money in)

(Important: the bank account must be yours - do not inform a third-party's bank account)

7 - Review everything you filled in your claim and then submit. In 5 to 7 days Cigna will reimburse you

Hope this is helpful! =)
