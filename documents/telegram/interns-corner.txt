--- Conversation 32 (2023-08-07) ---
User_bfd47a: be careful on the “cold messaging” part on Linkedin. You don’t want to hit up a recruiter that is not hiring for your position to ask about it 😅 Also, make the messages intentional, objective, and personalized. Talk about something they posted that drew your attention, bc if you just ask for a coffee chat saying you are interested to learn more about their role the odds are they might have 100 other messages like that in their inbox. If you are unsure who to reach out to, the events Vlad mentioned can help you figure that out! Also, emailing the company with a question about your role and seeing who responds

Potentially creating a role that doesn't exist or they already closed
